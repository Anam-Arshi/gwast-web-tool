Convert Observed-scale Heritability to Liability-scale

Convert observed-scale SNP heritability (h²obs) to liability-scale heritability for a disease trait.
Formula: h²_liability = h²_obs × [K(1-K)/Z²] × [K(1-K)/P(1-P)] (Reference)

Heritability Conversion Inputs
Estimated SNP-heritability on the observed scale, between 0 and 1.
Ratio of cases in your study sample (e.g., cases/(cases+controls)). Value between 0 and 1.
Estimated trait prevalence in the general population. Value between 0 and 1.
Standard error for observed-scale heritability (optional).
